How and why littlpaws was developed ?

littlePaws a website where ou can find your companion or find a foster for a companion. This idea hit our mind when we found a kitten with no mother around and no siblings so we decided to take it to our home until we find a foster for the kitten. Days passed and we were unable to find any foster we talked to our friends we came in contact with some social media platform which weren't find a foster home.That's how a actual idea to a project idea turned littlePaws
