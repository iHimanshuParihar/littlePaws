littlePaws Motive :

We understand how difficult the pet adoption process may be. With our initiative, we have made some progress in resolving this issue. Although many people want to adopt a pet, the adoption procedure can be difficult and sometimes result in applicants getting refused. This creates barriers between humans and animals, contributing to the overpopulation in shelters. littlePaws  tries to connect more people with pets in their neighbourhood, with the goal of lowering the number of animals that are homeless and providing them a shelter and humans a pet friend.

Approximately 1478 dogs & cats die every day on road in India. littlePaws is on a mission to provide every dog and cat a home before 2035. It’s just one of the many ways littlePaws! gives back and helps you become a part of something larger. Join littlePaws Community and help setting up Pet houses in your surrounding for strays.
